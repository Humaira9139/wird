Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

وِرْد (Wird) is a daily dhikr and adhkar app. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Dhikr counter progress and completion state
  • Streak history (days you completed your adhkar sessions)
  • Notification preferences and reminder times
  • App theme and display settings

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded, shared, or accessible to us or any third party.

Location

The app requests your approximate location solely to calculate local prayer times. Your location is used on-device only and is never stored or transmitted. You can deny location access and enter your city manually instead.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

وِرْد does not use any analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact

Questions? Reach us at support@wird.app
